Facilities & Services

At Balai Alegria, every detail of your stay is designed for relaxation and enjoyment. From fun activities and family-friendly games to dining and lounging, our amenities ensure that every guest finds something to enjoy.

Swimming Pool & Lounge Areas

Dive into our refreshing pool and relax on our lounge beds. Whether for a morning swim or an evening dip, the pool is the centerpiece of relaxation at Balai Alegria.

Café de Alegria & Bar

Enjoy freshly prepared meals, snacks, and drinks at our Glass Café & Bar. From floating breakfasts to cocktails by the pool, our café offers unique dining experiences in a cozy setting.

Cabana & Entertainment

Spend quality time with friends and family in our cabanas equipped with Smart TV, videoke, chess, junior billiards, and plenty of board games. Relax in hammocks or enjoy air coolers and fans for comfort.

Outdoor Fun & Games

Stay active with our wide range of activities: zipline, trampoline, volleyball, badminton, soccer, darts, billiards, air hockey, foosball, golf putty, swing, playhouse, and children’s playground. Perfect for all ages!
